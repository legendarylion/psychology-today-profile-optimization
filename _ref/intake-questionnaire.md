# **How This Questionnaire Works**

This questionnaire is practice-level, not clinician-only, and is collected in four stages that match the client journey. Each stage builds on the last with no redundant questions. Data collected here feeds every downstream deliverable: directory profiles (Psychology Today, Zencare, Zocdoc, and Private Practice Directory listings), website copy, SEO, paid ads, and social content.

Stage 1 is completed by the MHM sales team during discovery. Stage 2 is completed by the client after payment, before the kickoff call. Stage 3 is completed at or after kickoff. Stage 4 briefs are issued only for purchased services. The Clinician Roster in Stage 2 maps one-to-one to the Private Practice Directory profile schema — every roster field is a listing field.

*Field type legend: SELECT (single choice), MULTI-SELECT, CHECKBOX (select all), RADIO (yes/no or short single choice), TEXT (short answer), TEXTAREA (long answer), NUMBER, URL, EMAIL, PHONE, FILE (upload), REPEATER (repeatable group). Indented items are conditional follow-ups.*

# **Stage 2 — Practice Questionnaire**

Completed by the client after down payment, before the kickoff call. This is the core dataset: it powers positioning, copy, and every directory listing.

## **2.1  Services Offered**

•    **CHECKBOX**  Individual therapy / Couples therapy / Family therapy / Group therapy / Assessments & evaluations / Medication management / Intensives or retreats / Coaching or consulting / Other (text)

◦    **REPEATER**  IF GROUP THERAPY — For each group: name, focus, format (open/closed, in-person/virtual), schedule, current openings

•    **TEXTAREA**  Are there any services you plan to add, expand, or reduce in the near future?

## **2.2  Who You Serve**

•    **CHECKBOX**  Client types you currently work with — Adults / Teens / Children / Couples / Families / Older adults / Other (text)

•    **CHECKBOX**  Issues you are primarily known for helping with — Anxiety / Depression / Trauma & PTSD / Relationship issues / ADHD / Stress & burnout / Grief / Substance use / OCD / Eating disorders / Perinatal & postpartum / Men's issues / Faith & spiritual concerns / Life transitions / Chronic illness / Neurodivergence / Other (text)

•    **TEXTAREA**  In your clients' own words, how do they describe what brings them in — before they have clinical language for it? (e.g., “I can't stop overthinking,” “I feel like I'm too much,” “we keep having the same fight”)

•    **TEXTAREA**  If you could fill your schedule with more of one type of client or service right now, what would it be?

•    **TEXTAREA**  Are there clients or issues you do NOT work with or typically refer out? (We'll use this to filter who your marketing attracts)

## **2.3  Positioning & Differentiation**

•    *This section produces the strongest profile and website copy. Specific stories beat general claims.*

•    **TEXTAREA**  Describe your ideal client: their situation, what they've already tried, and what they're hoping will be different this time

•    **TEXTAREA**  What makes your practice genuinely different from other practices in your area? (Training, approach, lived experience, results, philosophy)

•    **TEXTAREA**  What's a common misconception about therapy — or your specialty — that you find yourself correcting?

•    **TEXT**  What 3–5 words do clients use to describe working with you?

•    **TEXTAREA**  Words, phrases, or tones you LOVE seeing in your marketing

•    **TEXTAREA**  Words, phrases, or tones to AVOID (e.g., “journey,” “safe space,” clinical jargon, anything that feels salesy)

•    **TEXTAREA**  Is there anything you want to avoid emphasizing or promoting?

## **2.4  Fees & Payment**

•    **REPEATER**  For each service: Service name / Session length / Standard fee

•    **CHECKBOX**  Payment options accepted — Insurance / Private pay / Medicare or Medicaid / EAP programs / Sliding scale / Superbills (out-of-network) / Session packages / Other (text)

◦    **MULTI-SELECT**  IF INSURANCE — Which insurance plans do you accept?

◦    **TEXTAREA**  IF SLIDING SCALE — Eligibility criteria and fee range

◦    **TEXTAREA**  IF PACKAGES — Describe package pricing (e.g., $170/hr for 3 sessions, $160/hr for 6\)

•    **RADIO**  Should fees be displayed publicly on profiles and listings? — Yes, exact fees / Yes, as a range / No
